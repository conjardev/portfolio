WEX Placement @ IBM

July 2024

In July of 2024, I was privilged to work for one week at IBM's Research and Development laboratory in Hursley, United Kingdom as part of a work experience placement.

Over the course of my week, I was given my own office and projects to work on. One project included learning to setup a piece of in-house software on a Virtual Machine and also testing documentation.

I was at the Hursley campus 9-5 every day for a week, where I also learned lots about life in the office.
