Writing & Video Production via YouTube

December 2023 - November 2024

In December of 2023, I began writing and planning a video documentary on one of my favourite video games Watch_Dogs 2.

Over the year that I worked on the project, I learned lots about writing, audio recording, and video prodction. I also had to create a sucessful marketing strategy to ensure that the video could find an audience - this included working with other creators, and members of the games cast.

For the video, I also conducted an interview with Shawn Baichoo, one of the actors from the game. For this I had to originally find then communicate professionally with his agent.

The video released on November 15th 2024, the 8 year anniversary of Watch_Dogs 2's release.
